## Resolution (HAO-357)

Status: Fixed

The `except Exception: pass` block at line 112 was reading a PID file and silently
discarding any I/O or parsing errors. This was a genuine bug: permission errors,
corrupted PID files, or race conditions on `/tmp/ipfs_kit_daemon.pid` would silently
produce no output, making daemon status diagnostics unreliable.

Fix applied: narrowed the catch clause to `(OSError, ValueError)` (covering file-read
failures and non-integer content respectively) and replaced the silent `pass` with a
user-visible message: `print(f"   PID: (unreadable: {e})")`. This keeps the status
command non-fatal while surfacing the actual error for operators.
